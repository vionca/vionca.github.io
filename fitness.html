How I ended in the fitness world

When I was four years old I started playing tennis, doing flamenco, and dancing ballet at the same time. My mom wanted me to stay with dancing and music, but my passion was tennis; I had always dreamed of playing professionally. When I was 17 years old, I was injured in both of my knees; the damage was such that it did not let me play to the same way i had played when I was younger. Thus, I decided to keep going, but in a different way: my motivation now was learning about other physical activities that wouldnâ€™t hurt my knees. I started reading and informing myself about the fitness world. I am excited to share some routines that I created that really help me to have a healthy body without pain.
