About Vionca Entertainment

Founded in 2018 by Vivianna Onorato, Vionca Entertainment is a personal lifestyle blog that was created with one goal in mind: to encourage, inspire and get inspired by all the people around the globe trough fashion contents, lifestyle tips, runway trend, and more of the entertainment world. Here, we talk about all things entertainment: art, fashion, music, and fitness. Through its channels, it creates daily content to inspire its audience.

I am excited to start this journey and keep all my readers engaged with my stories and my daily experiences.
